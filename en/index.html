Description

We present for sale an exceptional two-level apartment with an area of 90 m² located in a quiet part of Kraków, at Henryka i Karola Czeczów 28/36 street.

Apartment Layout

The apartment is spread over two levels, which gives it unique character and functionality:

  • First level: spacious living room with kitchenette, bathroom with shower, bedroom, balcony access, entrance hall
  • Second level: walk-through room, large bathroom with bathtub, wardrobe, main bedroom

Location

The property is located in a quiet and green area, while providing excellent access to the city center. Nearby you will find:

  • Bus stop - 650 meters
  • City rail stop - 500 meters (15 minutes to city center)
  • Primary School - 1200 meters
  • Nursery - 800 meters

Additionally, the area has numerous shops, restaurants and recreational areas, making this location ideal for families and people who value convenience and comfort of living.

Air Conditioning

The apartment is equipped with modern air conditioning on both floors, ensuring excellent living conditions throughout the year. The efficient air conditioning system allows for effective cooling of the apartment on hot summer days and serves as an excellent supplement to heating in winter. This solution guarantees thermal comfort regardless of the season and weather conditions.

Two Bathrooms

An exceptional feature of the apartment is having two fully equipped bathrooms. The first, on the ground floor, is a practical bathroom with a modern shower, ideal for daily morning routines. The second, upstairs, is a spacious bathroom with a bathtub, where you can relax after a long day. This solution eliminates bathroom queues in the family, provides privacy and significantly increases the comfort of daily apartment use.

Ideal for families who value comfort and convenience, and people looking for a quiet location with quick access to the city center and easy access to Kraków's ring road.

Amenities

Modern amenities: The apartment is equipped with advanced systems ensuring the highest comfort of use throughout the year.

Internet Installation

The apartment is equipped with professional structured cabling with Category 6 (Cat6) Ethernet wiring. Each room has a dedicated network socket, ensuring maximum flexibility in home network organization.

The installation allows free choice of fiber optic provider from several available options in the building. Thanks to the extensive Cat6 network, optimal placement of WiFi access points and connection of devices requiring stable wired connection is possible.

Główne zalety:

  • Wybór spośród kilku dostawców światłowodu
  • Okablowanie Ethernet Cat6 w każdym pokoju
  • Optymalne rozmieszczenie access pointów WiFi
  • Profesjonalna instalacja strukturalna
  • Idealne dla pracy zdalnej i smart home

System Wentylacyjny

The apartment is equipped with a modern mechanical ventilation system, ensuring constant fresh air supply and optimal humidity in the rooms.

Funkcje systemu:

Public Transport

Just a 5-10 minute walk from the apartment is a city rail stop, which allows quick and convenient access to Kraków city center in about 15 minutes.

Additionally, nearby is a small bus loop, providing connections to various parts of the city and convenient access to other public transport.

Inspiracje

Creating another non-walk-through room upstairs

You can easily close off the adjacent room upstairs by installing a partition wall with doors. This simple modification will allow you to create an additional, fully functional living space.

Thanks to this investment, you can increase the apartment's functionality by creating a private space that can serve as a bedroom, office or guest room. This solution does not require large financial or time investments.

Kliknij aby otworzyć w nowym oknie

The partition wall can be made from various materials - from lightweight plasterboard constructions to more solid masonry solutions. The remaining partition walls upstairs were made from Ytong blocks, which provide excellent acoustic and thermal insulation. The choice of material depends on individual needs and investment budget.

What is short-term rental?

Short-term rental is renting an apartment for periods from one night to several months, mainly to tourists and business travelers. It works similarly to a hotel, but in a private apartment. Popular platforms include Airbnb, Booking.com or Noclegi.pl. The owner usually receives higher rent than with standard rental, but it requires greater involvement in guest service.

Czym jest najem długoterminowy?

Najem długoterminowy to wynajmowanie mieszkania na okresy minimum kilku miesięcy, zazwyczaj od roku wzwyż. Najemcami są osoby szukające stałego miejsca zamieszkania - rodziny, single, pary czy studenci. Właściciel otrzymuje stały, przewidywalny czynsz co miesiąc, przy minimalnym zaangażowaniu w obsługę. W przeciwieństwie do najmu krótkoterminowego, koszty mediów zazwyczaj pokrywa najemca, a rotacja lokatorów jest znacznie niższa.

Neighborhood

🚆 Doskonała komunikacja kolejowa SKA

Location near the SKA railway junction guarantees fast, convenient and economical access to all of Kraków. Thanks to the modern agglomeration railway, you will reach the city center in record time, avoiding street traffic.

  • Fast connection to city center: Journey to Kraków Grzegórzki takes only 11 minutes by SKA train
  • Direct connection to airport: Access to Kraków-Balice Airport in about 30 minutes thanks to SKA1 line
  • Nowoczesny system kolejowy: SKA to jeden z najnowocześniejszych systemów transportu aglomeracyjnego w Polsce z 120 km tras
  • Alternative to traffic jams: While car travel can take 25-40 minutes, the train consistently takes 11 minutes
  • Economical solution: Travel cost significantly lower than maintaining a car in the city center

🌱 Pierwszy Park Złocień i zielone inwestycje

Ten rejon Krakowa przechodzi zieloną transformację. Pierwszy w okolicy Park Złocień to dopiero początek - miasto inwestuje setki milionów w zieleń, tworząc nowoczesną przestrzeń miejską przyjazną mieszkańcom.

  • Unique character: Park Złocień is the first park in this area of Kraków, opened in 2024
  • Strategic location: The park is a very important element in increasing the accessibility of green areas for residents
  • Part of a larger plan: Kraków allocated 211 million PLN in 2023 for investments in urban greenery
  • Pocket parks: Planned creation of 5 additional small parks with air-purifying vegetation
  • Improving quality of life: Greenery lowers temperature, improves air quality and increases residents' comfort

Rodziny z dziećmi mają dostęp do wyjątkowej infrastruktury edukacyjnej. Nowa szkoła podstawowa to prawdziwa perła architektoniczna z innowacyjnymi rozwiązaniami../

  • Kompletne wyposażenie: 24 sale lekcyjne, 4 nowoczesne pracownie przedmiotowe, hala sportowa z trybunami na 180 miejsc
  • Wysoki standard: Budynek przystosowany do potrzeb osób niepełnosprawnych, koszt inwestycji: 51,4 mln zł

🌊 Kompleksowe zabezpieczenia przeciwpowodziowe od 2017 roku

Thanks to investments worth over 130 million PLN, the area is fully protected against flooding. Modern retention and safety systems successfully passed tests during heavy rainfall in 2023.

Uregulowanie koryta rzeki Serafy (2017-2020)

  • Modernizacja w ramach inwestycji SKA: Kompleksowe uregulowanie koryta rzeki Serafy połączone z budową przystanku kolejowego za ponad 1 mld zł
  • New railway bridge: Construction of bridge over Serafa River with simultaneous riverbed correction (2018-2020)
  • Bank reinforcement: Organization and reinforcement of Serafa riverbed above and below new structures

Budowa muru ochronnego z grodzic winylowych (2022)

  • Nowoczesna technologia: Zainstalowanie 2 km ścian przeciwpowodziowych z grodzic winylowych na obu brzegach Serafy
  • Ochrona osiedla Złocień: Winylowa ściana o długości ponad 1 km na prawym brzegu rzeki za 2 mln zł (PGW Wody Polskie, 2022)
  • Wysokość zabezpieczeń: Mury o wysokości od 50 cm do 1 metra zapewniające bezpieczny przepływ wód powodziowych
  • Trwałość rozwiązania: Grodzice winylowe są całkowicie odporne na korozję i znacznie tańsze od tradycyjnych metod

Retention reservoir system in Bieżanów

  • Cztery zbiorniki ochronne: Serafa I (2015), Malinówka I, Malinówka II oraz Serafa II (do 2023)
  • Łączna pojemność: 336 tys. m³ wody retencyjnej chroniącej Złocień i Bieżanów
  • Investment cost: Nearly 30 million PLN for the construction of three new reservoirs (Polish Waters)
  • Effectiveness confirmed: The system successfully passed the test during heavy rainfall in May 2023
