Description

We present for sale an exceptional two-level apartment with an area of 90 m² located in a quiet part of Kraków, at Henryka i Karola Czeczów 28/36 street.

Apartment Layout

The apartment is spread over two levels, which gives it unique character and functionality:

  • First level: spacious living room with kitchenette, bathroom with shower, bedroom, balcony access, entrance hall
  • Second level: walk-through room, large bathroom with bathtub, wardrobe, main bedroom

Location

The property is located in a quiet and green area, while providing excellent access to the city center. Nearby you will find:

  • Bus stop - 650 meters
  • City rail stop - 500 meters (15 minutes to city center)
  • Primary School - 1200 meters
  • Nursery - 800 meters

Additionally, the area has numerous shops, restaurants and recreational areas, making this location ideal for families and people who value convenience and comfort of living.

Air Conditioning

The apartment is equipped with modern air conditioning on both floors, ensuring excellent living conditions throughout the year. The efficient air conditioning system allows for effective cooling of the apartment on hot summer days and serves as an excellent supplement to heating in winter. This solution guarantees thermal comfort regardless of the season and weather conditions.

Two Bathrooms

An exceptional feature of the apartment is having two fully equipped bathrooms. The first, on the ground floor, is a practical bathroom with a modern shower, ideal for daily morning routines. The second, upstairs, is a spacious bathroom with a bathtub, where you can relax after a long day. This solution eliminates bathroom queues in the family, provides privacy and significantly increases the comfort of daily apartment use.

Ideal for families who value comfort and convenience, and people looking for a quiet location with quick access to the city center and easy access to Kraków's ring road.

Investment Potential - Short-term Rental

What is short-term rental?

Short-term rental is renting an apartment for periods from one night to several months, mainly to tourists and business travelers. It works similarly to a hotel, but in a private apartment. Popular platforms include Airbnb, Booking.com or Noclegi.pl. The owner usually receives higher rent than with standard rental, but it requires greater involvement in guest service.

Investment Potential - Long-term Rental

What is long-term rental?

Long-term rental is renting an apartment for periods of at least several months, usually from a year up. Tenants are people looking for permanent residence - families, singles, couples or students. The owner receives stable, predictable rent every month, with minimal involvement in service. Unlike short-term rental, utility costs are usually covered by the tenant, and tenant rotation is significantly lower.

Market analysis: Q4 2024 - Kraków, Bieżanów-Prokocim

Long-term rental is a stable investment strategy for owners seeking predictable, regular income. It offers financial security with minimal time commitment from the owner.

Zalety tej strategii inwestycyjnej:

Neighborhood

🚆 Excellent SKA railway connection

Location near the SKA railway junction guarantees fast, convenient and economical access to all of Kraków. Thanks to the modern agglomeration railway, you will reach the city center in record time, avoiding street traffic.

  • Fast connection to city center: Journey to Kraków Grzegórzki takes only 11 minutes by SKA train
  • Direct connection to airport: Access to Kraków-Balice Airport in about 30 minutes thanks to SKA1 line
  • Modern railway system: SKA is one of the most modern agglomeration transport systems in Poland with 120 km of routes
  • Alternative to traffic jams: While car travel can take 25-40 minutes, the train consistently takes 11 minutes
  • Economical solution: Travel cost significantly lower than maintaining a car in the city center

🌱 First Złocień Park and green investments

This area of Kraków is undergoing a green transformation. The first Złocień Park in the area is just the beginning - the city invests hundreds of millions in greenery, creating modern urban space friendly to residents.

  • Unique character: Park Złocień is the first park in this area of Kraków, opened in 2024
  • Strategic location: The park is a very important element in increasing the accessibility of green areas for residents
  • Part of a larger plan: Kraków allocated 211 million PLN in 2023 for investments in urban greenery
  • Pocket parks: Planned creation of 5 additional small parks with air-purifying vegetation
  • Improving quality of life: Greenery lowers temperature, improves air quality and increases residents' comfort

🎓 The most modern school in Kraków

Families with children have access to exceptional educational infrastructure. The new primary school is a true architectural gem with innovative solutions../

  • Exceptional infrastructure: Primary School No. 20 opened in 2023 is one of the most modern educational facilities in Kraków
  • Innovative solutions: Sports field on the hall roof - unique architectural solution on the city scale
  • Complete equipment: 24 classrooms, 4 modern subject workshops, sports hall with stands for 180 seats
  • High standard: Building adapted for disabled persons, investment cost: PLN 51.4 million
  • Space for 600 students: Single-shift system ensuring optimal learning conditions

💨 Intensive efforts to combat nuisances

  • Comprehensive modernization: The city implements Odor Nuisance Reduction Program with budget exceeding PLN 100 million
  • Modern technologies: Installation of multi-stage odor removal system in Bieżanów collector (2023)
  • Facility sealing: Completed work on main odor sources - primary settling tanks and pumping stations
  • External support: National Environmental Protection Fund provides approximately PLN 100 million for further deodorization
  • Work progress: In 2019-2023, primary settling tanks were modernized and the Płaszów collector was sealed

🏠 High standards of new apartments in accordance with EU 2024 standards

  • EU 2024 Standards: All new buildings must meet highest energy standards introduced in 2024
  • Better acoustic insulation: New regulations from April 2024 require higher standards for apartment soundproofing
  • Energy certificates: All new apartments have mandatory energy efficiency certificates
  • Resident comfort: Modern ventilation systems, thermal modernization and energy management ensure better air quality

🌊 Comprehensive flood protection since 2017

Thanks to investments worth over 130 million PLN, the area is fully protected against flooding. Modern retention and safety systems successfully passed tests during heavy rainfall in 2023.

Serafa River bed regulation (2017-2020)

  • Modernization within SKA investment: Comprehensive regulation of the Serafa riverbed combined with railway station construction for over 1 billion PLN
  • New railway bridge: Construction of bridge over Serafa River with simultaneous riverbed correction (2018-2020)
  • Bank reinforcement: Organization and reinforcement of Serafa riverbed above and below new structures

Construction of protective wall with vinyl sheet piles (2022)

  • Modern technology: Installation of 2 km of flood protection walls made of vinyl sheet piles on both banks of the Serafa
  • Protection of Złocień estate: Vinyl wall over 1 km long on the right bank of the river for 2 million PLN (PGW Polish Waters, 2022)
  • Protection height: Walls ranging from 50 cm to 1 meter in height ensuring safe flood water flow
  • Solution durability: Vinyl sheet piles are completely corrosion-resistant and significantly cheaper than traditional methods

Retention reservoir system in Bieżanów

  • Four protective reservoirs: Serafa I (2015), Malinówka I, Malinówka II and Serafa II (until 2023)
  • Total capacity: 336 thousand m³ of retention water protecting Złocień and Bieżanów
  • Investment cost: Nearly 30 million PLN for the construction of three new reservoirs (Polish Waters)
  • Effectiveness confirmed: The system successfully passed the test during heavy rainfall in May 2023
